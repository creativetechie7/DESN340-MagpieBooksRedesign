Unfortunately, we do not accept used books. We encourage you to reach out to some of the incredible used book stores in Edmonton (Paper Birch, The Bookseller, The Wee Book Inn, and so many more!), or, if you are interested, we can donate some books on your behalf to a prison library.

Magpie Books works with two different organisations to get books into prisons (NorQuest College and The Gela Prison Project). We send books there on a monthly or bi-monthly basis. If you would like to contribute, both organisations accept paperback books (no hardcover books, please), of all genres; books featuring racialized or queer characters and writers are particularly in demand. If you have any further questions, please send an email to hello@magpiebooks.ca.
